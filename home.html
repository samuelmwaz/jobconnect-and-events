Our Vendors & Marketplace

Vendor Policy Highlights

The Job Connect and Events extends its services to support and regulate **all types of vendors**, including product vendors, service providers, and event-related vendors.

  • **Registration & Verification:** All vendors must complete a registration process, providing accurate business information and necessary certifications to ensure legitimacy and quality.
  • **Fees & Commission:** Vendors agree to a **percentage-based fee** for each successful transaction or service provided.
  • **Official Documentation:** The platform provides official documents for each transaction, serving as proof of agreement and recorded in our secure database.
  • **Dispute Resolution:** The Job Connect and Events will **mediate between the vendor and customer** to reach a fair resolution in cases of disputes.
  • **Partnership Benefits:** Registered vendors gain **increased visibility**, access to a wider customer base, and secure documentation.
